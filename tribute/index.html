Barış Manço

Barış Manço

Songwriter, composer, singer, TV producer, cultural ambassador

  • Born in 1943
  • 1962: First record, one of the founders of Anatolian Rock genre
  • 1970: First hit single "Dağlar Dağlar" sold over 700,000 copies
  • 1975: First studio album "2023"
  • Over 30 singles, dozen albums before his death in 1999
